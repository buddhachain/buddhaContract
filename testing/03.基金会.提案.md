## 3. 提案

### 3.1 创建

```bash
xchain-cli wasm invoke --fee 1000000 buddha --method make_proposal -a '{"key":"burn_rate", "newvalue":"20", "timestamp": "xxxxxx", "expire":"yyyyyy"}' --account XC1234567890200001@xuper

#先查找需要授权的key和value
xchain-cli wasm invoke --fee 1000000 buddha --method find_proposal -a '{"key":"burn_rate"}' --account XC1234567890200001@xuper

xchain-cli wasm invoke --fee 1000000 buddha --method approve_proposal -a '{"key":"burn_rate", "newvalue":"20", "timestamp": "xxxxxx", "operate":"0" }' --account XC1234567890200002@xuper
xchain-cli wasm invoke --fee 1000000 buddha --method approve_proposal -a '{"key":"burn_rate", "newvalue":"20", "timestamp": "xxxxxx", "operate":"0" }' --account XC1234567890200003@xuper

xchain-cli wasm invoke --fee 1000000 buddha --method find_proposal -a '{"key":"burn_rate"}' --account XC1234567890200001@xuper
xchain-cli wasm invoke --fee 1000000 buddha --method list_proposal --account XC1234567890200001@xuper

```
